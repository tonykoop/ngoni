Quick Start

Governing physics: Mersenne–Taylor (strings) + Helmholtz + skin radiator (bowl)
Total string load (Kamele 10): ~92 lbf at target tensions (safe for maple neck)
Bowl segments: 110 × 18° miter · 11 rings · 10 segments/ring
Lathe swing needed: 12 in minimum (Kamele 10), 18 in (Kamele 14)
Top risk: Bridge stability — verify break angle ≥ 10° before mounting skin
