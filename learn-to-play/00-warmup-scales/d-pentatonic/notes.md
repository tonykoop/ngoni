# D Pentatonic Scale Walk

**Provenance:** Original exercise.

**Scale:** D F♯ A D F♯ (D major pentatonic — omits G and C♯).

**How to play:**
- Section A: stepwise up and down the pentatonic scale, one octave.
- Section B: each scale degree held for a full beat — practice tone consistency
  at each position.

**Practice tips:**
- The fretless neck means every pitch must be squeezed from the correct spot
  by ear. Use a tuner app to check your intonation on the way up and down.
- Let string 5 (D3) drone freely under the entire exercise. The drone makes
  out-of-tune notes very obvious — use it as your feedback.
- Once comfortable, try sliding into each note from a semitone below. This
  is the ngoni's signature ornament.
