# Dusty Road

**Provenance:** Original composition by Heifer Zephyr for this ngoni build.
MIT licensed — free to perform, record, and arrange.

**Designed for:** Tony Koop's ngoni (5-string, D pentatonic).

**Range:** D3–F♯5. Full range of the instrument.

**Form:** AABB.

**Character:** A travelling tune — medium pace, purposeful, slightly yearning.
The title refers to the long roads of the Sahel where the ngoni tradition
travelled north and west from Mali.

**Practice tips:**
- The B section's peak (F♯5 — the highest string open) should ring clear and
  bright. If it sounds dull, check that you're plucking cleanly off the string
  without damping with your fingertip as you pull away.
- Loop the A section 4 times before adding the B section — the repetition is
  part of the tradition.
- At ♩=100 this is a comfortable walking pace. Don't push it faster until
  the slides feel natural.
